Our Story

A De Pere practice built on continuity and commitment.

Ledgeview Dental Care was already part of the De Pere community before Dr. Cole Stockheimer purchased the practice in 2019. He did not start from scratch. He stepped into an established patient base at 2225 Kaftan Way and built on it with advanced clinical training in four areas that most general dentists never pursue together.

Dr. Stockheimer earned his DDS from Marquette University School of Dentistry in 2011 and completed his undergraduate work at UW-Madison. He spent his early career in Madison and Milwaukee group practices before acquiring Ledgeview to put down roots in the community where he and his family live, just outside De Pere in Suamico.

The result is a practice where the owner is also the practitioner you see, where advanced credentials are not a brochure claim but a daily clinical reality, and where your care does not get passed to a chain or a corporate group.

Independent and locally owned

Ledgeview operates outside the DSO model that has consolidated much of Northeast Wisconsin dentistry. Dr. Stockheimer's ownership means clinical decisions stay with the dentist who trained for them, not a management company.
